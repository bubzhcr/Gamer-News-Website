The Sims 4 is a life simulation game where you create and control Sims, build homes, pursue careers, and shape their lives. The game offers a mix of creativity and gameplay, allowing players to shape their Sims’ lives and explore endless possibilities in a vibrant, interactive world. Available on PC, Mac, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X|S, you can buy it on the EA Games Store, Steam, and other major retailers.

A recent Sims 4 update has triggered a glitch in CAS. This was caused by slider settings. Check your game folders for expired mods and cc, and press on the link to the Reddit Sims 4 community to find player solutions online, for further advice.
